Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Do you take custom orders?

Yes! We love creating special orders for events, parties, and celebrations. Please contact us at least 48 hours in advance for custom orders.

Do you offer delivery?

Yes, we deliver within a 5km radius. Orders above ‚Çπ500 get free delivery. You can also order through popular food delivery apps.

Are your products suitable for people with dietary restrictions?

Our sourdough bread is naturally easier to digest due to fermentation. We're working on gluten-free and vegan options. Please call us to discuss specific dietary needs.

How long does your bread stay fresh?

Our sourdough stays fresh for 5-7 days when stored properly in a bread box or paper bag. Regular bread is best consumed within 2-3 days. All our products can be frozen for up to 3 months.

Do you offer catering services?

Absolutely! We provide catering for events, corporate meetings, and parties. Contact us at catering@butterandbloom.com for a custom quote.
